How to Play

Much like League of Legends, the game of Chess takes a day to learn but a lifetime to master. Conversely, the game of Pong takes five minutes to learn and a day master. We've decided to combine both of these incredibly influential and renowned games into one even more influential and renowned game: Ultra Chess 1987 Bronze Edition.

In simplest terms, the premise of UC1987BE is that you play both games at the same time. Pong is modified so there is one Cannon and one Paddle. While you move, you must fend off balls fired from the cannon. If the ball scores three times before you move, you lose your turn and the computer will move for you.

You can also play one of the alternate game modes. Instead of Pong, there will be a move timer. Make your move within 30 seconds, or else the game will move for you.

You can also disable both of these and play a simple game of networked chess. Click on the images above for tutorials on Chess and Pong.
